Experience
  • The Healing Lotus App is a clinical tool for both healthcare professionals and their clients. • It is a compilation of manual self care techniques, rehabilitative exercises and stretches for increased flexibility & mobility.
  • The aim is to visually educate & support our clients for a positive & effective rehabilitative outcome.
  • Using the app is very simple & interactive. You will be able to search grossly by body region or by viewing common broad conditions.
  • Due to The Healing Lotus' holistic philosophy, you will also find helpful links to some of our favourite health tools, e.g. Insight Timer (mindfulness app).
  • Professionals can create personal routines for their clients and select multiple videos from different categories. Sections for additional notes / instructions is also available alongside each exercise allowing for greater communication & understanding between therapists & clients.
  • PDF Charts / Blank pages can be created to track client progress over the course of a rehabilitation program. These files can also be downloaded & exported from the app to be uploaded / printed for storage in the clients file.
